Fijian Language Learning App

This application helps verify AI-generated Fijian translations. Native speaker validation ensures high-quality data used to train future AI models. Verified content is stored for use in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems that assist in understanding and teaching the Fijian language.

Each translation—whether a vocabulary word, phrase, or paragraph—is generated from scanned documents, news articles, and other sources. Human reviewers (like Makita) edit or confirm accuracy, and once verified, items become part of a growing dataset used to improve AI comprehension of Fijian.
